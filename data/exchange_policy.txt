Exchange Policy

TITLE: Exchange Eligibility Products can be exchanged within seven days
of delivery if the item is unused in original packaging and the
exchange reason is valid.

TITLE: Exchange Request Process Customers initiate an exchange request
through the order history page by selecting the item and choosing the
exchange option.

TITLE: Exchange Reasons Valid exchange reasons include wrong size wrong
colour defective product and incorrect item delivered.

TITLE: Exchange Pickup and Delivery The original item is picked up by
the logistics partner and the replacement is dispatched simultaneously
or within two business days.

TITLE: Exchange Availability Exchange is subject to product availability
in the requested variant at the time of the exchange request.

TITLE: Exchange Price Difference If the exchanged item has a higher
price the customer must pay the difference before the replacement is
dispatched.

TITLE: Exchange Refund for Lower Price If the replacement item is priced
lower than the original a refund for the difference is issued to the
original payment method.

TITLE: Exchange Inspection The returned item from an exchange is
inspected at the warehouse before the replacement is confirmed.

TITLE: Exchange Rejection Exchanges may be rejected if the product
shows signs of use damage or missing components upon inspection.

TITLE: Exchange for Defective Products Customers receiving defective
products can opt for an exchange or a full refund within the eligible
window.

TITLE: Exchange Notification Customers receive confirmation of the
exchange request approval and estimated delivery of the replacement.

TITLE: Exchange Restrictions Certain product categories including
personalised items perishables and digital products are not eligible
for exchange.

TITLE: One Exchange Per Order Each product can be exchanged only once
per order and repeat exchange requests for the same item are not
permitted.

TITLE: Exchange Documentation Customers may be required to submit
photographs of the product to support the exchange request.

TITLE: Exchange During Sale Items purchased during flash sales or
clearance events may have restricted or no exchange eligibility.

TITLE: International Order Exchange Exchanges are not available for
internationally shipped orders due to customs and logistics constraints.

TITLE: Exchange Escalation If an exchange request is rejected
customers may escalate to the resolution team through the support
portal.

TITLE: Exchange Tracking Customers can track the status of their
exchange including pickup and replacement delivery on the order page.

TITLE: Exchange and Loyalty Points Loyalty points earned on the
original purchase are retained during an exchange unless a refund is
processed.

TITLE: Exchange Policy Review The exchange policy is reviewed
periodically and customers are notified of changes with adequate
notice.
