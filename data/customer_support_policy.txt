Customer Support Policy

TITLE: Support Availability Customer support is available seven days a
week from eight in the morning to ten at night across all time zones.

TITLE: Support Channels Customers can reach support via live chat email
phone and the in-app help centre.

TITLE: Response Time Standard support queries receive a response within
twenty four hours and urgent issues within four hours.

TITLE: Support Ticket Creation Customers can raise a support ticket
through the help centre and receive a unique ticket number for tracking.

TITLE: Priority Support Premium account holders receive priority support
with faster response times and dedicated agents.

TITLE: Support Escalation If a support agent cannot resolve an issue
within two business days the ticket is escalated to a senior team.

TITLE: Support Language Availability Support is available in English
Hindi and ten regional languages to assist customers effectively.

TITLE: Live Chat Support Live chat is available during business hours
and connects customers with an agent within three minutes.

TITLE: Email Support Email support handles complex queries that require
documentation review and detailed investigation.

TITLE: Phone Support Phone support is available for urgent issues
including failed deliveries payment problems and account security.

TITLE: Support Bot Assistance An AI-powered support bot handles common
queries instantly before routing complex issues to human agents.

TITLE: Support Feedback After each interaction customers are invited to
rate their support experience to help improve service quality.

TITLE: Support Knowledge Base A self-service knowledge base is available
with articles guides and FAQs for common issues.

TITLE: Support for Accessibility Customers with accessibility needs can
request specialised support assistance through the help centre.

TITLE: Support Ticket Closure Tickets are automatically closed after
seventy two hours of inactivity following resolution.

TITLE: Support Data Privacy All support interactions are recorded and
stored securely in compliance with data protection regulations.

TITLE: Support During Peak Periods During sales seasons response times
may be slightly longer due to high volumes of incoming queries.

TITLE: Third Party Seller Support For issues related to third party
seller products customers should contact the seller directly through
the platform messaging system.

TITLE: Support for Business Accounts Business account holders have
access to a dedicated account manager for ongoing support.

TITLE: Support Complaint Resolution Formal complaints about support
quality can be submitted to the customer relations team for review.
