Account and Security Policy

TITLE: Account Creation Customers must provide a valid email address and
phone number to create an account on the platform.

TITLE: Account Verification New accounts are verified through a one-time
password sent to the registered email or mobile number.

TITLE: Password Requirements Passwords must be at least eight characters
long and include a combination of letters numbers and special characters.

TITLE: Password Reset Customers can reset their password through the
forgot password option using their registered email or phone number.

TITLE: Two Factor Authentication Two-factor authentication is available
and strongly recommended for enhanced account security.

TITLE: Account Login Attempts Accounts are temporarily locked after five
consecutive failed login attempts to prevent unauthorised access.

TITLE: Session Management Active sessions are automatically logged out
after thirty minutes of inactivity on web and mobile platforms.

TITLE: Device Management Customers can view and remove trusted devices
from the security settings section of their account.

TITLE: Account Suspension Accounts violating platform policies may be
suspended pending investigation without prior notice.

TITLE: Account Deletion Customers may request permanent account deletion
through the account settings page subject to pending order completion.

TITLE: Data Access on Deletion Upon account deletion personal data is
removed within thirty days subject to legal retention obligations.

TITLE: Suspicious Activity Alerts Customers receive real-time alerts for
unusual login activity or unauthorised account access attempts.

TITLE: Third Party Login Customers may sign in using Google or Facebook
accounts subject to those platforms' authentication policies.

TITLE: Account Transfer Account ownership cannot be transferred to
another individual or entity under any circumstances.

TITLE: Minor Accounts Users below eighteen years of age must have
parental consent before creating and operating an account.

TITLE: Account Merging Multiple accounts belonging to the same user
cannot be merged and duplicate accounts may be suspended.

TITLE: Security Breach Response In the event of a security breach
affected accounts are locked and customers are notified immediately.

TITLE: Phishing Awareness Customers are advised not to share login
credentials and to report phishing attempts through the support portal.

TITLE: Account Inactivity Accounts with no activity for twenty four
months may be deactivated after prior notification to the registered
email.

TITLE: Business Account Security Business accounts have additional
security controls including role-based access and audit log monitoring.
