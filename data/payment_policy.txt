Payment Policy

TITLE: Accepted Payment Methods Customers can pay using credit cards
debit cards UPI net banking and digital wallets.

TITLE: Payment Authorization Payment gateways verify transaction details
before confirming the order.

TITLE: Payment Confirmation Customers receive confirmation once the
payment is successfully processed.

TITLE: Payment Failure Failed payments may occur due to insufficient
balance or network issues.

TITLE: Payment Retry Customers can retry failed payments from the order
page.

TITLE: UPI Payments UPI payments require approval from the customer
mobile banking application.

TITLE: Card Payments Card payments are processed through secure payment
gateways.

TITLE: Net Banking Net banking allows customers to pay directly through
their bank accounts.

TITLE: EMI Payments Certain products support installment payments
through eligible credit cards.

TITLE: Wallet Payments Digital wallets allow quick payments using stored
balances.

TITLE: Payment Security Transactions are protected using encryption and
fraud monitoring systems.

TITLE: Payment Fraud Detection Suspicious transactions may be flagged
for manual review.

TITLE: Payment Refund Handling Refunds for cancelled orders are
automatically returned to the original payment method.

TITLE: Payment Chargebacks Customers may initiate chargebacks through
their bank for disputed transactions.

TITLE: Payment Transaction Logs Payment systems maintain logs of all
successful and failed transactions.
