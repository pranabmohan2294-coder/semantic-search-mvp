Fraud and Abuse Policy

TITLE: Fraud Detection Systems The platform uses automated fraud
detection systems to identify suspicious transactions and account
behaviour in real time.

TITLE: Order Fraud Indicators Orders flagged for fraud include
mismatched billing and delivery addresses unusually high order values
and multiple failed payment attempts.

TITLE: Account Takeover Prevention Customers are alerted via email and
SMS when a new device or location is used to access their account.

TITLE: Chargeback Fraud Customers who repeatedly initiate illegitimate
chargebacks may have their accounts suspended and be reported to
payment networks.

TITLE: Refund Abuse Customers misusing the refund process by returning
used or damaged products or making false claims are subject to account
action.

TITLE: Discount Code Abuse Bulk purchasing using discount codes for
resale purposes is prohibited and may result in order cancellation and
account suspension.

TITLE: Fake Review Fraud Submitting fake reviews incentivised reviews
or using multiple accounts to manipulate ratings is strictly prohibited.

TITLE: Identity Fraud Using another person's identity payment details
or account credentials to make purchases is a criminal offence and
will be reported to authorities.

TITLE: Seller Fraud Sellers engaging in counterfeit sales fake
listings or payment manipulation will be permanently banned and may
face legal action.

TITLE: Bot and Automated Purchasing The use of bots scripts or
automated tools to purchase limited products or exploit flash sales is
prohibited.

TITLE: Referral Fraud Creating fake referrals or self-referrals to earn
loyalty points or referral bonuses is considered fraud and points will
be reversed.

TITLE: Fraud Reporting Customers who suspect fraud or have experienced
fraudulent activity can report it through the dedicated fraud
reporting form.

TITLE: Investigation Process Fraud reports are investigated within five
business days and customers are informed of the outcome and any actions
taken.

TITLE: Account Freeze During Investigation Accounts under fraud
investigation may be temporarily frozen to prevent further transactions
pending resolution.

TITLE: Legal Action The platform reserves the right to pursue legal
action against individuals engaged in proven fraudulent activity.

TITLE: Fraud Prevention Education Customers are provided with resources
and tips on identifying and avoiding fraud including phishing and
impersonation scams.

TITLE: Collaborative Fraud Prevention The platform collaborates with
payment networks banks and law enforcement to prevent and address
fraud.

TITLE: Fraud Liability Customers who knowingly participate in fraud
are liable for any financial losses incurred by the platform or third
parties.

TITLE: Fraud Policy Review The fraud and abuse policy is reviewed
quarterly to address emerging fraud patterns and update prevention
measures.

TITLE: Whistleblower Protection Employees or sellers reporting internal
fraud in good faith are protected under the platform's whistleblower
policy.
