Product Listing and Quality Policy

TITLE: Product Listing Standards All products listed on the platform
must meet minimum quality standards and comply with applicable
regulations.

TITLE: Product Description Accuracy Sellers are required to provide
accurate product descriptions including dimensions weight materials
and specifications.

TITLE: Product Image Requirements Product images must be clear
high-resolution and accurately represent the item being sold.

TITLE: Prohibited Products Certain product categories including
counterfeit goods hazardous materials and restricted substances are
prohibited from listing.

TITLE: Product Authenticity Sellers must guarantee the authenticity of
branded products and provide proof of authorisation when required.

TITLE: Quality Inspection Random quality inspections are conducted on
products stored in platform fulfilment centres.

TITLE: Product Reviews Customer reviews are verified against confirmed
purchases to maintain authenticity and prevent fake reviews.

TITLE: Review Moderation Reviews violating community guidelines
including offensive language or irrelevant content are removed after
moderation.

TITLE: Product Rating System Products are rated on a five-star scale
based on verified customer reviews and purchase feedback.

TITLE: Seller Ratings Sellers are rated based on product quality
delivery speed and customer service metrics.

TITLE: Product Recalls In the event of a safety recall affected
customers are notified and offered full refunds or replacements.

TITLE: Counterfeit Reporting Customers can report suspected counterfeit
products through the product listing page using the report option.

TITLE: Product Compliance Products must comply with BIS standards and
other applicable regulatory requirements before listing.

TITLE: Listing Removal Non-compliant or policy-violating product
listings are removed within twenty four hours of identification.

TITLE: Product Variants Listings with multiple variants such as size
colour or material must accurately reflect stock availability for each
variant.

TITLE: Hazardous Material Handling Products classified as hazardous
require special packaging handling and shipping compliance.

TITLE: Intellectual Property Sellers must not list products that
infringe on trademarks copyrights or patents of third parties.

TITLE: Product Expiry For consumable products sellers must ensure
adequate shelf life at the time of dispatch and listing.

TITLE: Nutritional Information Food and health products must display
accurate nutritional information and ingredient lists on the listing
page.

TITLE: Product Update Obligations Sellers must update product listings
immediately when specifications pricing or availability changes occur.
