Pricing and Discount Policy

TITLE: Price Accuracy The platform strives to display accurate prices
but reserves the right to cancel orders where pricing errors occur.

TITLE: Dynamic Pricing Prices may vary based on demand location and
time of purchase for certain product categories.

TITLE: Price Match Policy The platform does not currently offer price
matching against competitor websites or offline stores.

TITLE: Discount Codes Discount codes can be applied at checkout and are
valid for a specified period and product category.

TITLE: Discount Code Stacking Only one discount code can be applied per
order unless explicitly stated during a promotional event.

TITLE: Sale Pricing Products listed during sales events are sold at
discounted prices for a limited period only.

TITLE: GST and Taxes All prices displayed include applicable GST and
taxes unless otherwise indicated at checkout.

TITLE: Price Drop Policy If a product's price drops within forty eight
hours of purchase customers may request a price adjustment credit.

TITLE: Bulk Pricing Discounts Bulk purchases above a specified quantity
threshold may qualify for additional negotiated discounts.

TITLE: Employee Discounts Employees are eligible for special discount
rates on eligible products subject to internal policy terms.

TITLE: Student Discounts Verified students may access exclusive
discounts through the student programme after verification.

TITLE: Cashback Offers Cashback offers are provided by partner banks and
payment platforms and are governed by their respective terms.

TITLE: Flash Sale Pricing Flash sale prices are valid only for the
duration of the sale and cannot be claimed after the event ends.

TITLE: Price Visibility Prices are displayed in the local currency of
the customer and converted at the prevailing exchange rate.

TITLE: Subscription Pricing Subscription plans are billed at a fixed
rate for the subscription period and are not subject to mid-cycle
changes.

TITLE: Bundle Pricing Bundled product combinations are priced at a
discount compared to purchasing individual items separately.

TITLE: Price Notification Customers can enable price drop alerts for
wishlisted products to receive notifications when prices change.

TITLE: Discount Fraud Prevention Misuse of discount codes including
sharing or reselling is prohibited and may result in account suspension.

TITLE: VAT for International Orders International orders may attract
additional VAT or import duties based on destination country regulations.

TITLE: Price History Customers can view historical pricing trends for
eligible products to make informed purchasing decisions.
