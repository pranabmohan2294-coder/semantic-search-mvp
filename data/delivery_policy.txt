Delivery Policy

TITLE: Delivery Timeline Standard delivery usually takes two to five
days depending on the shipping location.

TITLE: Order Dispatch Orders are dispatched from the warehouse within
twenty four hours after payment confirmation.

TITLE: Shipment Tracking Customers receive a tracking number once the
order is handed over to the logistics partner.

TITLE: Delivery Attempt Policy Delivery partners typically attempt
delivery up to three times before returning the package.

TITLE: Delivery Address Accuracy Incorrect addresses may delay delivery
or result in the order being returned to the warehouse.

TITLE: Same Day Delivery Same day delivery is available only for select
cities and orders placed before the daily cutoff time.

TITLE: Delivery Confirmation Customers may need to provide an OTP or
signature when receiving high value items.

TITLE: Delivery Delays Weather conditions or transportation disruptions
can extend delivery timelines.

TITLE: Failed Delivery If the recipient is unavailable the courier may
attempt redelivery on the next business day.

TITLE: Delivery Notifications Customers receive notifications when
orders are dispatched, out for delivery, and delivered.

TITLE: International Shipping International deliveries may require
customs clearance and additional documentation.

TITLE: Delivery Charges Shipping fees depend on distance weight of the
package and chosen delivery speed.

TITLE: Delivery Partner Responsibilities Logistics partners must
maintain package safety and update delivery status accurately.

TITLE: Weekend Deliveries Weekend deliveries may be available in metro
areas depending on courier availability.

TITLE: Delivery Issue Reporting Customers can report delivery problems
through the support portal within forty eight hours.
