Privacy Policy

TITLE: Data Collection The platform collects personal information
including name email address phone number and delivery address to
process orders and provide services.

TITLE: Data Usage Customer data is used to fulfil orders personalise
recommendations and improve platform performance.

TITLE: Data Sharing Personal data is not sold to third parties and is
shared only with logistics partners and payment processors necessary
for order fulfilment.

TITLE: Data Storage Customer data is stored on secure servers with
encryption and access control measures in place.

TITLE: Data Retention Customer data is retained for a period of three
years after the last transaction or account activity.

TITLE: Cookie Policy The platform uses cookies to track user sessions
preferences and browsing behaviour to improve user experience.

TITLE: Data Access Rights Customers have the right to access their
personal data stored on the platform by submitting a data access
request.

TITLE: Data Correction Customers can update incorrect personal
information directly from their account settings page.

TITLE: Data Deletion Customers may request deletion of their personal
data by closing their account subject to legal retention requirements.

TITLE: Third Party Links The platform may contain links to third party
websites whose privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

TITLE: Marketing Communications Customers can opt out of promotional
emails and SMS by updating their communication preferences in account
settings.

TITLE: Data Breach Notification In the event of a data breach customers
will be notified within seventy two hours in compliance with
regulations.

TITLE: Children Privacy The platform does not knowingly collect data
from users under the age of eighteen without parental consent.

TITLE: Location Data Location data may be collected to enable delivery
tracking and provide localised services to customers.

TITLE: Data Portability Customers may request a copy of their data in a
portable format by contacting the data protection officer.

TITLE: Consent Management Customers can manage their data consent
preferences through the privacy settings in their account dashboard.

TITLE: Analytics Data Anonymised and aggregated data is used for
analytics and business reporting without identifying individual users.

TITLE: Privacy Policy Updates The privacy policy is reviewed annually
and customers are notified of significant changes via email.

TITLE: Data Protection Officer Customers with privacy concerns can
contact the designated data protection officer through the help centre.

TITLE: Regulatory Compliance The platform complies with applicable data
protection laws including GDPR and local data privacy regulations.
