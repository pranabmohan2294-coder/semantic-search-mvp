Refund Policy

TITLE: Refund Eligibility Window Customers may request a refund within
seven days of delivery if the product is unused and in original
packaging.

TITLE: Refund Processing Time Approved refunds are credited within five
to seven business days after the returned item passes inspection.

TITLE: Refund Request Method Refund requests must be submitted through
the order history page in the customer account dashboard.

TITLE: Refund for Damaged Products If a product arrives damaged,
customers should upload images of the item and packaging during the
refund request.

TITLE: Refund for Incorrect Items Customers receiving the wrong item can
request either a replacement shipment or a full refund.

TITLE: Refund for Cancelled Orders Orders cancelled before shipping are
refunded automatically to the original payment method.

TITLE: Refund Verification Support teams verify refund requests using
delivery confirmation, warehouse inspection, and payment records.

TITLE: Refund Payment Method Refunds are issued to the same payment
method used during the purchase unless otherwise required by regulation.

TITLE: Partial Refund Policy Partial refunds may apply when returned
products are missing accessories or promotional bundles.

TITLE: Refund Rejection Conditions Refunds may be rejected if the item
shows signs of heavy use or damage caused by misuse.

TITLE: Refund Fraud Prevention Repeated refund requests from the same
account may trigger fraud review and additional verification.

TITLE: Refund Status Tracking Customers can track refund progress
directly from the order details page.

TITLE: Refund Customer Notification Email and app notifications are sent
when refund requests are approved or rejected.

TITLE: Refund Escalation Customers may escalate refund disputes to the
resolution team if the initial review is rejected.

TITLE: Refund Processing Delays Bank processing times may extend refund
completion beyond the standard processing window.
