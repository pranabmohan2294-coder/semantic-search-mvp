Cancellation Policy

TITLE: Cancellation Eligibility Customers may cancel an order before it
is dispatched from the warehouse without any cancellation fee.

TITLE: Cancellation Window Orders can be cancelled within two hours of
placement if payment has been confirmed and dispatch has not begun.

TITLE: Cancellation Request Method Cancellation requests must be
submitted through the order history page in the customer account
dashboard.

TITLE: Cancellation After Dispatch Orders that have already been
dispatched cannot be cancelled and must go through the return process
after delivery.

TITLE: Cancellation Refund Timeline Refunds for cancelled orders are
processed within three to five business days to the original payment
method.

TITLE: Partial Order Cancellation Customers may cancel individual items
from a multi-item order before dispatch without cancelling the entire
order.

TITLE: Cancellation by Seller In rare cases sellers may cancel orders
due to stock unavailability and customers will receive a full refund
automatically.

TITLE: Cancellation Notification Customers receive an email and app
notification confirming the cancellation and expected refund timeline.

TITLE: Cancellation Fee Exceptions Orders cancelled after dispatch or
during peak sale periods may attract a nominal cancellation handling fee.

TITLE: Repeated Cancellations Accounts with a high frequency of
cancellations may be flagged for review and subject to purchase
restrictions.

TITLE: Pre-Order Cancellation Pre-orders can be cancelled any time
before the product is released and dispatched.

TITLE: Subscription Cancellation Subscription orders must be cancelled
at least twenty four hours before the next billing cycle to avoid
charges.

TITLE: Cancellation During Promotional Periods Orders placed during
flash sales or promotional events may have restricted cancellation
windows.

TITLE: Cancellation Escalation If a cancellation request is not
processed within the expected timeline customers may escalate through
the support portal.

TITLE: Cancellation and Loyalty Points Loyalty points earned on
cancelled orders will be reversed upon cancellation confirmation.

TITLE: Cancellation Documentation Customers do not need to provide a
reason for cancellation but feedback is encouraged to improve services.

TITLE: Cancellation Impact on Discounts Discount codes or coupons used
on cancelled orders are reinstated only if the cancellation is within
the eligible window.

TITLE: Bulk Order Cancellation Bulk or wholesale orders require direct
coordination with the business team for cancellation approvals.

TITLE: Cancellation and Gift Orders If a gift order is cancelled the
refund is issued to the original purchaser and not the recipient.

TITLE: Cancellation Policy Updates The company reserves the right to
update cancellation policies and customers will be notified in advance.
