Seller Policy

TITLE: Seller Onboarding Sellers must complete identity verification
business registration and tax compliance checks before listing products.

TITLE: Seller Agreement Sellers must accept the platform's seller
agreement which outlines rights responsibilities and commission
structures.

TITLE: Commission Structure The platform charges a commission on each
sale which varies by product category and is deducted before seller
payouts.

TITLE: Seller Payout Schedule Seller payouts are processed weekly after
deducting platform commissions returns and applicable fees.

TITLE: Seller Performance Metrics Sellers are evaluated on order
fulfilment rate return rate customer ratings and response time.

TITLE: Seller Account Suspension Sellers failing to meet minimum
performance standards may have their accounts suspended after warnings.

TITLE: Inventory Management Sellers are responsible for maintaining
accurate inventory levels and updating stock availability in real time.

TITLE: Order Fulfilment Obligations Sellers must dispatch orders within
the committed handling time after payment confirmation.

TITLE: Seller Return Handling Sellers must accept returns for eligible
products and cooperate with the platform's return and refund process.

TITLE: Prohibited Seller Practices Sellers must not engage in price
manipulation review manipulation or any deceptive listing practices.

TITLE: Seller Dispute Resolution Sellers may raise disputes regarding
returns chargebacks or commission deductions through the seller portal.

TITLE: Seller Training Resources Onboarding guides webinars and
documentation are available to help sellers optimise their performance.

TITLE: Brand Registry Verified brand owners can enrol in the brand
registry programme for enhanced listing protection.

TITLE: Seller Communication Sellers must respond to customer queries
routed through the platform within twenty four hours.

TITLE: Tax Compliance Sellers are responsible for collecting and
remitting applicable GST on their sales in accordance with tax laws.

TITLE: Seller Product Liability Sellers bear liability for product
defects injuries or damages caused by their listed products.

TITLE: Seller Intellectual Property Sellers must not use unauthorised
brand names logos or trademarks in their product listings.

TITLE: Exclusive Seller Arrangements Sellers may enter into exclusive
arrangements with the platform for specific product categories subject
to negotiation.

TITLE: Seller Data Access Sellers have access to sales analytics
customer feedback and performance dashboards through the seller portal.

TITLE: Seller Policy Updates Sellers are notified of policy changes with
a minimum of thirty days advance notice before implementation.
